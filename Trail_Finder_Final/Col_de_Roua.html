Col de Roua

About the trail

The pass is located on the border between France and Italy . It connects, from south to north, Bardonecchia ( metropolitan city of Turin ) to Modane ( Savoy ). It has the shape of a wide saddle between the Gran Bagna (3,080 m ) to the southwest and the Punta Nera (3,046 m ) to the east. During Antiquity, the Col de la Roue was one of the busiest passages between the Susa valley and the Maurienne ; Coins from ancient Rome have been found nearby .
