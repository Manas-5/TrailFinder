Cap d'Antibes

About the trail

The Cap d'Antibes hiking trail is located along the scenic French Riviera. The Cap d'Antibes area is known for its exclusive villas but also for its lush Mediterranean vegetation and stunning coastal views. This trail winds around the peninsula of Antibes, offering hikers breathtaking views of the azure waters of the CÃ´te d'Azur and the surrounding landscapes. The trail starts near the town of Antibes, and stretches approximately 5 kilometers, making it a perfect half-day hike.

Recommended Gear

Comfortable walking shoes are recommended. Also, water and sunscreen, especially during the warmer months.
