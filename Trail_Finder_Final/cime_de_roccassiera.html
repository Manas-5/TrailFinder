Cime de Roccassiéra

About the trail

The Cime de Roccassièra trail is a captivating hiking route located in the Alpes-Maritimes region of France, leading to the summit of Cime de Roccassièra. This trail is known for its challenging terrain and the spectacular panoramic views it offers from the top. Reaching the summit offers a rewarding experience with breathtaking 360-degree views of the French Riviera and the Italian Alps on clear days. The hike is considered moderate to challenging due to its steep inclines and rocky paths, making it a favorite among more experienced hikers. The Cime de Roccassièra trail is approximately 10 kilometers round trip, and the difference in altitude is 1060 meters.

Recommended Gear

Sturdy hiking boots are essential for this trail. Due to the elevation of the hike, weather-appropriate clothing is crucial, layers are recommended to adjust to changing temperatures.
