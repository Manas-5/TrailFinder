Trail finder is your new best friend when it comes to exploring and traversing your area. If you are interested in walking your nearby trails, you are a nature enjoyer or if your favourite activity is looking at stunning views from mountain tops then Trail Finder will help you organize your trip, give ideas and let you connect with your trail goer friends.

With the latest version of your app you will be able to explore your nearby area through a search link where you can find an endless amount of hikes to explore, with all the information needed.
