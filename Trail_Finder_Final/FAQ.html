Frequently Asked Questions

I am lost in the website and can't find my way to the home page

If you are in this position there is no need to worry. You are always one click away from the home page. You just have to press our logo on the top of the navigation bar.

How to login to the website

In order to login to the website you have to press the login button on the home page and register a new account. If you already have an account just enter your details in the specified location.

How to reset my password?

Unfortunately right now there is no interactive way to change your details. In order to do so you have to send an email to our support team.

How can i find hikes if i dont know to name of a hike?

If you dont know the name of the place you are interested in going there is no need to worry. You just have to go to the Explore area in the Menu navigation button. There you can browse all our hike and choose your next adventure.

I am new to hiking, do i need to have any spetial equipment to be able to paricipate?

No special equipment is necessary for you to start exploring your nearby trails. For all the information about equipment you can go to our hiking tips page in the menu navigation button. There you can find all the information necessary about tips, equipment and much more to help you plan your next trip even more effectively.

Where can i see my favorite hikes?

All your favorite hikes are accessable through your account. Press your icon to see your profile page. There, you can add and remove all trails from your favorites.

Where can I manage my past trips?

In order to view all your past trips you have to go to your profile page, there you can see a list where you can add and remove hikes you have already completed. You can click on the bar and add one of your trips to that list.
