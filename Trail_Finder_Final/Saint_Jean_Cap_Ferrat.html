About the trail

The Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat hiking trail is a gem nestled on the peninsula of Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat in the French Riviera. Renowned for its luxurious villas and pristine natural beauty, this area offers a tranquil escape with its beautiful views of the Mediterranean Sea. Highlights along the trail include passing by the famous Villa Ephrussi de Rothschild. Recommended Gear

Comfortable walking shoes are recommended. Also, water and sunscreen, especially during the warmer months.
